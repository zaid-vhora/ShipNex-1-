FAQ

Common Questions

Where can I find my tracking number?

Your tracking number is included in your confirmation email, SMS notification, and printed on your shipping receipt. It typically begins with "SHP-" followed by the year and a unique identifier.

How often is tracking information updated?

Tracking information is updated in real-time as your package moves through our network. Updates occur at each scan point, typically every few hours during transit.

What does "In Transit" mean?

"In Transit" means your package is currently moving through our delivery network. It has been picked up and is on its way to the destination. Check the timeline for specific location updates.

My tracking shows no updates. What should I do?

Tracking updates may take 24 hours to appear after pickup. If your tracking number was just generated, please wait and check again. If it's been more than 24 hours without updates, please contact our support team.

Can I change my delivery address?

Yes, address changes may be possible before final delivery. Contact our customer service team immediately with your tracking number and the correct delivery address.

What if my package is delayed?

Delays can occur due to weather, customs, or other factors. Check your tracking for updates and estimated delivery changes. Contact support if you need immediate assistance.
