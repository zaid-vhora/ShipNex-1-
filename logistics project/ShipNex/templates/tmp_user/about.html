Our Story

Building the Future of Logistics

Shipnex was founded in 2009 with a simple yet ambitious vision: to make global shipping accessible, reliable, and efficient for businesses and individuals alike.

What started as a small regional courier service has grown into a comprehensive logistics platform serving over 150 countries. Our journey has been driven by innovation, customer focus, and an unwavering commitment to excellence.

Today, we handle millions of shipments annually, from small parcels to complex freight operations. Our success is built on the trust of our customers and the dedication of our team of over 5,000 logistics professionals worldwide.

Our Journey

Milestones

Key moments that shaped Shipnex into what it is today.

2009 - Foundation

Shipnex was founded in Mumbai with a vision to transform regional logistics.

2013 - National Expansion

Expanded operations across India, establishing 50+ regional hubs nationwide.

2017 - Global Reach

Launched international shipping services, reaching 50+ countries worldwide.

2020 - Digital Platform

Unveiled our advanced digital platform for seamless online booking and tracking.
